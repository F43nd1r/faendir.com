[Multitool](https://github.com/F43nd1r/Multitool) is a [Lightning Launcher](https://www.lightninglauncher.com/wordpress/) plugin bundling various functions, including 

- Animations
- Scheduled Backups
- Gesture Support
- Music Widget
- Calendar Widget
- Script Manager
